Notre Histoire

A place to slow down,
enjoy something real,
and feel transported.

Petite Orangerie is a French-inspired glacerie and pâtisserie opening in Salt Lake City. We make everything from scratch with ingredients we believe in — real cream, real fruit, real chocolate, real vanilla. No artificial flavors. No corn syrup. No shortcuts.

Our menu is built around the orange — Valencia, blood orange, bergamot, orange blossom — alongside French classics like Valrhona chocolat noir, Sicilian pistache, and caramel au beurre salé with fleur de sel from Guérande.

We offer naturally vegan sorbets, dairy-free oat milk options, and gluten-free macarons — not as afterthoughts, but as core to who we are.

Real Ingredients

Valrhona chocolate, Sicilian pistachio, Boiron fruit purées, Madagascar vanilla. Every ingredient chosen for quality, never for convenience.

For Everyone

Naturally vegan sorbets. Dairy-free oat milk ice cream. Gluten-free macarons. Options for every family member — no compromises, no asterisks.

Handcrafted Daily

Small-batch ice cream churned every morning. Macarons piped by hand. Madeleines baked fresh. Nothing from a factory, everything from our kitchen.
